Information & Policies

Assigned Training Groups / Skill Level:

Am I in the Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced class?

Athletes participate in Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced classes. Classes listed on the calendar as “OPEN” are available to any of these levels and to “Advanced” Fly-Kids.

Skill Levels are assigned by coaching staff. If you need to know which skill level/class group you will participate in prior to registering, please Contact us.

Class Schedule:

Consult the DCVault.com training calendar for current training quarter details. Look for the training group/class of interest to view times, location, etc.

ALWAYS check the schedule before attending your assigned class in case of changes due to weather or other factors.

Reserving your Class Time Slots(Zen Planner):

24 hours after registering, athletes receive email instructions on how to activate their Zen Planner account (used to reserve classes and track how many sessions are remaining).

Athletes may only reserve classes that correspond to their assigned skill level on Zen Planner.

Reserve well in advance to get the time slots you prefer. If a class time is full, you can add yourself to a “Wait List” and check back to see if a slot opened before the class starts.

Note: Reservations, modifications, or cancellations can only be made on Zen Planner 24 hours before a class begins!

Weather Cancellations and Calendar Updates:

Note:The Zen Planner Calendar is NOT used for Weather Updates!.

Zen Planner is ONLY used for reserving your class times and may not reflect a last-minute class cancellation or time change!

Refer to the DCVault.com website calendar one-two hours prior to your class for any potential updates.

The website calendar reflects the most current and up to date schedule. We never “plan” to cancel class. Any inquiries related to schedule updates will be directed to the website calendar.

90% of the time any change to the schedule will be posted 2-hours or more in advance. No changes will be made to the class schedule within 1-hour of class.

If a class is cancelled or cut short due to weather, a credit will be applied to your class package.

If a class you reserved is held and you chose not to attend the class, it will be charged against your account.

Equipment + Poles:

DC Vault provides all needed equipment during scheduled training classes, including poles, weights, etc.

Spikes:

Spikes Are NOT Permitted during practice unless the coaching staff instructs otherwise.

ONLY 1/8" are permitted at the DC Vault Home Training Facility located at 2100 East Capitol Street NE, DC (these are NOT the 1/4” spikes that come with new shoes). If you are participating in a competition at the DC Vault site, ensure you have the correct 1/8” spikes prior to competition or you will be disqualified.
