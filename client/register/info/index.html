Reserving your Class Time Slots:

24-48 hours after registering, athletes will receive an email with instructions on how to activate/access their complimentary Zen Planner account, which is used to reserve your class time slots and to track how many classes you have available on your training package. All classes must be reserved through Zen Planner more than 24 hours before a class begins.

Reserve well in advance to ensure you get the class slots you prefer!

Changes can be made to your Zen Planner reservations up until the 24-hour window before a class begins. You can add yourself to a “Wait List” if a class is full and check back to see if a slot opened up before the class starts.

Weather Cancellations - Calendar Updates:

NOTE – the Zen Planner Calendar is NOT used for Weather Updates! Zen Planner is ONLY used for reserving your class times and may not reflect a last-minute class cancellation or time changes.

IF you are looking for potential weather cancellations, refer to the DCVault.com website calendar one-two hours prior to your class. The website calendar reflects the most current and up to date intention. We never “plan” to cancel class. Any inquiries related to possible schedule changes will be directed to the website calendar.

90% of the time any change to the schedule will be posted 2-hours or more in advance. No changes will be made to the class schedule within 1-hour of class.

If a class is rained out mid-session, a credit will be applied to your Zen Planner class package. If you choose not to attend a class that you have registered for, the class will be charged against your account if it was not cancelled.

Assigned Training Groups / Skill Level:

Am I in the Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced class?

Athletes participate in All Ages or Fly-Kids Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced class sessions. All Ages “OPEN” classes are available to any of these levels with the exception of Fly-Kids Beginner-Intermediate athletes. Advanced Fly-Kids may attend an All Ages “OPEN” class.

Athletes are assigned their training group by coaching staff. If you need to know which skill level group you will participate in prior to registering, please Contact us.

Athletes are ONLY able to reserve class time slots that correspond to their skill level group, and will only see the classes they are eligible for on their Zen Planner class reservation calendar.

Class Schedule:

For specific schedule details on a current or upcoming training course, please consult the DCVault.com training calendar. Look for the training group of interest and click on the time slot to view details (date, time, location, course duration, etc…).

The schedule for an upcoming training quarter is typically posted one month in advance.

ALWAYS check the schedule before attending your assigned class in case of changes due to weather or other factors.

Class Location:

Classes are primarily offered at the DC Vault Training Facility, located at 2100 East Capitol Street, NE Washington, DC.

Classes may also be offered seasonally at one of one of our partner facilities. During registration the available/active facilities will appear for selection once you have chosen your desired group and course.

The location for a class can always be viewed in the training calendar by clicking on any scheduled class and opening the details.

Equipment:

DC Vault provides all needed equipment during scheduled training classes, including poles, weights, etc.

Spikes:

Spikes Are NOT Permitted during practice unless the coaching staff instructs otherwise.

Absolutely no spikes longer than 1/8” are permitted at the DC Vault Home Training Facility located at 2100 East Capitol street NE, DC! If you are participating in a competition at the DC Vault site, ensure you have the correct 1/8” spikes prior to competition (these are not the ¼” spikes that come with shoes).
