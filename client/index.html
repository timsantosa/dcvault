DC VAULT

DC Vault is the Premiere Pole Vault club in the Washington, DC Metro Region. Serving the District of Columbia, Virginia, Maryland, Delaware and Pennsylvania, DC Vault provides year-round training for beginner through elite-level athletes.

Through a variety of partnerships, DC Vault provides training at 7 facilities in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. Each facility has been uniquely outfitted with a wide array of specialized equipment provided by the club, allowing for training targeted to athletes of all skill levels.
