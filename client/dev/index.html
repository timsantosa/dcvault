DC VAULT

DC Vault is the Premiere Pole Vault club in the Washington, DC Metro Region. Serving the District of Columbia, Virginia, Maryland, Delaware and Pennsylvania, DC Vault provides year-round training for beginner through elite-level athletes.

Meet DC VAULT

President / Head Coach

Edward Luthy

Edward Luthy - President / Head Coach

Coach Luthy’s experience spans over 30 years as a pole vault coach. During that time he has instructed countless athletes at all levels of competition.

His current and former athletes include
two Olympic Trials Finalists (men's 1996 and women's 2012),
the 2013 Masters World Champion (60-65 group),
three NCAA Division I National Championships Finalists,
four NCAA Division I Conference Champions,
five NCAA Division I University Record Holders,
three NCAA Division I University Stadium and Fieldhouse Record Holders,
two NCAA Division II University Record Holders,
one NCAA Division III University Record Holder,
five USATF Youth National Champions,
one USATF Youth National Record Holder,
and Multiple High School Conference, State and Local record holders.

Coach Luthy believes that strict adherence to step by step technical progression provides each athlete with the greatest opportunity to obtain their maximum potential and perform consistently throughout their competitive career. Though he has developed his own training system, it's roots are based in in-depth research of Russian pole vault programs, established by Vitali Petrov (renowned coach of the greatest male and female vaulters in history - Sergei Bubka and Yelena Isinbayeva). Through overseas travels to Petrov's Italian training site, to comparative analysis of nearly every technical model variant known to date, Coach Luthy's training system continually evolves.

A self-trained pole vaqulter, Coach Luthy cleared a competitive PR of 17’1" (5.20m) before succumbing early on to an Achilles tendon injury. He competed on a full scholarship for Western Michigan University from 1999-2001, the All Army Track Team from 1993-1995 and earned varsity letters at 3 different high schools.

Head Coach / Baltimore Operations

Stephanie Colby

Stephanie Colby - Head Coach / Baltimore Operations

Coach Colby joined DC Vault in the spring of 2011 and currently leads our Baltimore training programs.
Prior to joining DC Vault, Coach Colby was an elite level competitor, ranking top ten in the nation in the women’s pole vault in 2001 with a personal record of 14’ 1.25”.

During this time she trained with some of the nation’s leading pole vault coaches, including Bob Farley (PV Hall of Fame award winner and Olympic Development Coach) and Earl Bell (Former Olympian and founder of Bell Athletics). She has also trained with Olympians Kellie Suttle, Jillian Swartz and Erica Bartolina. Coach Colby was the 2x North American Pole Vault Association Grand Prix Champion, 3x USA National Competitor and represented the USA in Japan and Taiwan.

During her collegiate years Coach Colby set the University of Rhode Island indoor and outdoor women’s pole vault record, as well as the Atlantic-10 Conference indoor and outdoor records. Additionally, she was the 3x New England Champion, 4x Atlantic-10 Champion, ECAC Champion, Penn Relays Champion and a finalist at the 1999 NCAA Indoor National Championships.

Additionally, Coach Colby is the current defending Masters National Pole Vault Champion. Coach Colby’s supporting background in athletics includes a B.S. in physical education and exercise science, competitive gymnastics training and high level success as a collegiate Pentathlete.

Assistant Coach / Web Developer

Timothy Santosa

Timothy Santosa - Assistant Coach / Web Developer

Coach Tim joined the DC Vault Level I program as a high school sophomore in 2012. Progressing quickly, he was offered a Level III training slot with the DC Vault Emerging Elite group in 2014. Despite injury, he successfully claimed 3 Regional Championship titles, 1 County Championship title and took Gold at the Maryland 4A State Championships with a height of 13'6" in 2015.

Going on to attend the University of Maryland as a Computer Science major, Coach Tim continued his training in the DC Vault Emerging Elite program and began instructing Level I and II athletes in 2016. Since then, he has taken on increasingly complex coaching duties and become a core member of the DC Vault team.

Additionally, Coach Tim manages the DC Vault social media accounts, supports video production and is the current webmaster for DCVault.com
